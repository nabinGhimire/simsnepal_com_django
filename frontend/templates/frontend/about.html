Our Mission

Our goal is to modernize educational administration in primary, secondary, and higher secondary schools across Nepal. By transitioning legacy paper ledgers and fragmented offline files into a secure, fast, and unified cloud framework, we help schools improve operational efficiency.

Through our seamless SSO integration with the Hamro Business framework, school staff can securely share user access permissions across administration, financials, and library systems effortlessly.

Our Vision

We envision a digitized future for Nepalese schools where academic statistics, grades, routines, and presence metrics are immediately and securely accessible. Digital workflows enable administration departments to focus less on records bureaucracy and more on improving classroom teaching standards.

With highly robust local server hosting setups and lightweight, performance-optimized codes, our platform loads instantly on mobile, tablet, and desktop systems even in low bandwidth environments.
