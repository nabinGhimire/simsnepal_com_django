Frequently Asked Queries

Find answers to common integration, support, and administrative questions about our school portals.

How does Hamro Business SSO login operate?

+

SIMS Nepal integrates directly with the Hamro Business SSO architecture. When clicking 'SSO Partner Login', you are securely redirected to the central authentication domain. Once successfully logged in, our system validates the session payload using robust HMAC cryptographic keys and creates/registers your active portal account seamlessly.

Is student record data private and secure?

+

Absolutely. Academic transcript data, student registration keys, and contact records are securely isolated. Our hosting systems use industry-standard SSL/TLS configurations, daily backups, and robust permission check matrices to ensure data remains strictly confidential.

Can parents track attendance updates via SMS?

+

Yes. Our attendance module features automated real-time SMS pipelines. When administrative staff marks a student absent, our system can instantly send absent bulletins to saved parent/guardian phone logs, helping maintain excellent school-home transparency.

Does the pricing plan support custom municipal needs?

+

Yes. If you represent regional government ward schools or require specific grading reports mapped to Nepalese secondary education outlines, reach out via our contact inquiry module. We offer customized packages and dedicated local assistance.
